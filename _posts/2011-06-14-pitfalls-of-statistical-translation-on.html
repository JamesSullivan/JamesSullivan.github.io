--- layout: post title: Pitfalls of Statistical Translation on a Large Scale date: '2011-06-14T16:15:00.000-07:00' author: Sully categories: ML tags: - ML - Statistical Translation modified_time: '2023-01-11T05:25:27.324-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-7835395644559909128.post-8095646173232774445 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.solutions.asia/2011/06/pitfalls-of-statistical-translation-on.html --- An interesting article from the Atlantic about one of the shortcomings of statistical based translation, which seems to be hitting a wall of diminishing returns in its ability to improve, at least for the way Google is currently implementing it. Google is dropping an automatic-translation tool because overuse by spam-bloggers is flooding the Internet with sloppily translated text which in turn is making computerised translation trained on translated text available on the Internet even sloppier.
